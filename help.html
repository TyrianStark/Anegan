Help & FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I invite Anegan to my server?

    Click the "Invite Anegan" button on the home page and follow the OAuth2 authorization prompts.

  • Does Anegan store user data?

    Only minimal server data is stored for bot functionality, logging, and settings. All sensitive data is handled securely. Additional information is available on the Terms & Conditions page

  • What do I do if a command doesn’t work?

    Ensure the bot has proper permissions and roles. If issues persist, report them in the support server.

  • Can I request a new feature?

    Yes! Join the support server to submit feature requests or report bugs.
